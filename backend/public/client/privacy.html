Privacy Policy

Data protection and platform privacy disclosures.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Information we collect

We may collect customer identity details, contact information, delivery addresses, order preferences, gifting notes, and support conversation details when you browse, inquire, or place an order.

How information is used

Information is used to process orders, personalize gifting recommendations, manage delivery coordination, improve storefront performance, prevent misuse, and support customer service.

Browser cookies

Cookies may support authentication, cart continuity, analytics, performance monitoring, and remembered preferences. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some storefront functions may become limited.

Encryption and security

We use reasonable administrative and technical controls designed to protect data in transit and restrict operational access. No digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

Data sharing

We may share limited details with delivery partners, payment processors, customer support vendors, and legal authorities when required to complete services or comply with obligations.

Customer choices

You may request updates, corrections, or deletion of eligible personal information by contacting our support team through the contact page.
